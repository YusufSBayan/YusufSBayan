FEDERASI ICE SKATING INDONESIA

Federasi Ice Skating Indonesia (FISI) / Indonesia ice Skating Federation is a non-profit National Sport Organization in Indonesia founded in 2016 and staffed by volunteers to serve the needs of Indonesia growing communities of recreational and competitive Ice Skaters.
